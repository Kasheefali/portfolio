What is Fashion Photography ?

Fashion images, strictly speaking, is dedicated to showing clothing and other fashion items in the best possible terms. At its roots, it is marketing. It uses deeply ingrained human psychology to create an interest in and a desire for a product. Fashion images are trendy, hip, sexy, and vibrant. .

Fashion photographers use beautiful models, avant-garde modeling poses, and exotic locations to help communicate the brand story and promote the designer's goods. But not all fashion is the high-end stuff. Open any clothing magazine, and you will see everyday people wearing everyday clothing, and yes, those are fashion photos too.
